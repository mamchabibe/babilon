Concept

The city as a puzzle system

The theme frames the platform as a rebuilt digital Babylon: a city of gates, towers, records, signals, sealed archives, and coded roads. Players do not simply “visit a website”; they enter a mythic network and advance from threshold to threshold.

A

Public mystery

The landing page creates atmosphere, trust, and intrigue. It introduces the world, gives the first hints, and hosts the official truth of the game.

B

Controlled progression

Players register, enter teams, unlock levels, submit answers, and move through a carefully designed sequence instead of wandering chaotically.

C

Living signal network

Announcements, clues, milestone reveals, and new gates can all be broadcast from one official system while keeping gameplay coherent.

Progression

The player journey

1. Hear the signal Players discover the project through the landing page, a broadcast channel, or an invitation post.
2. Pass the first gate Registration and identity creation turn curiosity into formal entry.
3. Join a house or team Participants are grouped for healthier collaboration, shared problem solving, and better moderation.
4. Solve the threshold puzzles Early levels filter serious players while teaching the logic language of the hunt.
5. Unlock the deeper city Meta puzzles, coded archives, account trails, and multi-part clues open the main season.

Experience pillars

What the theme should feel like

  • Majestic, ancient, and secretive — not horror, not chaos.
  • Elegant interfaces with cryptic language and clear player direction.
  • Enough mystery to feel elite, enough structure to remain playable.
  • A sense of gates, archives, towers, maps, signals, and hidden routes.
  • Every solved puzzle should feel like opening a sealed chamber of the city.
