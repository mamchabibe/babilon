Some paths are not broken. Some paths are withheld. If you arrived here by accident, return to the known roads. If you arrived here on purpose, remain still and read what has been left behind.

I

First notice

The gate is closed, but closure is not the same as absence. A sealed passage may still be waiting for the correct hour, the correct name, or the correct witness.

II

Record of entry

It is said that those who reach the hidden state are marked once in light and once in silence. Only one of those marks can be seen from the outside.

III

Unissued instruction

When the eastern channel begins to hum, do not answer immediately. Wait for the second echo. The first belongs to the city. The second belongs to the one who called you.
