To all who hear this message,

I am Alastor — ألستور.

For a long season, the world hath slept beneath noise, distraction, and the forgetting of greater things. Truth hath been broken into fragments, scattered among the careless, hidden beneath symbols, silence, and dust. Yet not all have lost the will to seek.

And so I call unto you.

This day marketh the launch of the trial.
The path is now opened.
The signs are now set.
The game hath begun.

Think not that ye have entered a pastime for the idle. Nay — this is a road for the watchful, for the steadfast, for those whose minds do not faint before mystery, nor whose hearts tremble before the unknown.

Ye shall be tested.
Ye shall be led through shadow and sign, through riddle and fragment, through things forgotten and things yet to be revealed. Many shall look, but few shall perceive. Many shall begin, but few shall endure unto the end.

If there remain among you those worthy to gather what was scattered, let them rise now.

Seek the first clue.
Mark well what is spoken.
Trust not appearances.
And remember this: every fragment hath a place, and every step draweth you nearer to that which hath long awaited your coming.

Enter, then.

The game is launched.
The path is awake.
And I, Alastor, shall be watching.

Proceed.
