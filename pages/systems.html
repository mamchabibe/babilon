Operational fragments

Unread directives

Not every mechanism is meant to be understood at first sight. Some signals are left visible on purpose, for those who know how to read what has not yet been explained.

Custodian seal

The city does not belong to the crowd. Its locks answer to one name alone, and every gate that wakes does so beneath the hand of Alastor, whether the watchers notice it or not.

OwnerAlastor
ClaimThe final key stays in one hand
WarningNothing opens without leave

Signal chamber

When the broadcast begins, do not search the streets below. The voice will rise from the chamber above the eastern ring, where glass remembers every word and sends it back changed.

Broadcast pointThe chamber above the eastern ring
ConditionWait for the seventh tone
Echo ruleOnly the marked will hear it clearly

Silent directives

Archive this and read it later: the red window must not be opened before the lower hall goes dark, the third ledger must remain unnumbered, and the ninth witness is never to be named aloud.

Directive IKeep the red window sealed
Directive IILeave the third ledger uncounted
Directive IIIDo not speak of the ninth witness
