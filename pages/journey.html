Rule book

The Rules of the Ascent

Welcome, seekers. You do not enter this path by chance. You enter because something in you answered the call. Read carefully. Those who ignore the rules rarely climb far.

Rules I-V

1. Gather points. Each trial you solve will grant you points. Some rewards are known. Others are not.
2. Move quickly. Time has value here. The earliest teams to break a level may receive greater rewards.
3. Not all rewards are visible. There are things hidden in these halls. Some secrets may grant points to those sharp enough to find them.
4. Paths do not remain open forever. Certain gates, routes, or opportunities may close after a time. Those who wait too long may find the road changed.
5. Every answer must be earned. Random guessing will not guide you upward. Observe. Think. Connect. Then act.

Rules VI-X

6. Respect the game. Any attempt to break, bypass, exploit, or corrupt the path is forbidden. Those who do so may be cast out from the ascent.
7. The order of things is not yours to know. You will not be told how many trials lie ahead. You will know only the next gate before you.
8. The first trial begins with value. The first level is worth 100 points. Beyond that, the weight of each ascent may change.
9. Only the worthy continue. To solve is to advance. To advance is to prove yourself.
10. Watch carefully. Nothing is ever placed without reason.

Now enter.
Climb well.
And do not mistake mercy for weakness.

- Alastor
